This project provides a comprehensive analysis of global disaster events and response efforts from 2018 to 2024. The Power BI dashboard, **Disaster_response_analysis.pbix**, offers a multi-page interactive experience to explore trends, impacts, and responses to various disasters. ## ğŸ“Š Key Insights from the Dashboard

- **Dominant Disaster Types:** Water-related disasters (floods, tsunamis) are the most frequent, while wind-related events (hurricanes, tornadoes) incur the highest economic costs.
- **Geographical Hotspots:** Southeast Asia and North America are the most disaster-prone regions, with specific "corridors" showing a high recurrence of events.
- **Response Time Analysis:** A 15% improvement in average response time since 2018 correlates with a 10% reduction in casualties in comparable events. Urban areas receive aid 40% faster than rural ones.
- **Resource Allocation Gaps:** A persistent gap in aid allocation is observed in sub-Saharan Africa, which receives 25% less funding relative to the impact severity compared to other regions.
- **Temporal Patterns:** A clear seasonal trend is visible, with a spike in disasters in the Northern Hemisphere during the summer and autumn months.
